About Jacob Elordi

Jacob Elordi is an Australian actor born on June 26, 1997, in Brisbane, Queensland, Australia. He first gained international attention through Netflix's hit romantic film series The Kissing Booth, where he portrayed Noah Flynn.

He later received worldwide recognition for his performance as Nate Jacobs in HBO's award-winning television series Euphoria. His powerful performances have established him as one of the most respected young actors of his generation.

Jacob has continued expanding his acting career with major films including Saltburn, Priscilla, Frankenstein, and The Narrow Road to the Deep North.

Beyond acting, Jacob is admired for his professionalism, dedication, humility, and passion for storytelling. His ability to portray complex characters has earned praise from audiences and critics worldwide.
